Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Manifest collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application. By using Manifest, you acknowledge and agree to this policy.

Data Collection

We may automatically collect the following information:

  • Crash logs and diagnostic data
  • App usage analytics (via Apple Analytics)
  • Device specifications
  • Mood and alignment check-in responses

Your manifestations are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers unless you enable cloud sync.

Data Usage

We use collected data to:

  • Monitor app performance
  • Diagnose bugs or crashes
  • Understand user behavior to improve the app
  • Enhance the manifestation experience

All analytics data is anonymized. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Manifest may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store — for app distribution and payments
  • Apple Analytics — for anonymized usage metrics

No personally identifiable information is stored on external servers.

Privacy Controls

Creating an account is not required to use Manifest. All your manifestations remain private and stored on your device. If you wish to request deletion of any analytics data associated with your device, please contact us.

Children's Privacy

Manifest is rated 4+ and does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. Parents and guardians are responsible for monitoring their children's use of the app.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@manifest.app
